South China University of Technology (SCUT) is one of China’s key universities operating under the direct leadership of the State Ministry of Education. The university, that encompasses a total area of 294 hectares, is located in Guangzhou, a southern metropolitan in China. The university has 2 campuses such as The Wushan Campus and Guangzhou Higher Education Mega Center Campus. South China University of Technology (SCUT), generally known as the South China Institute of Technology, was established in 1952.
In 1960, South China University of Technology was awarded ranked among the key universities of China. In 1981, it had been approved by the State Council to grant doctor degrees and master’s degrees, and was among the primary universities in the country to do so. In 1995, it had been selected as one of the universities that China will build vital investment within the 21st century.
In 1999, it had been rated “Excellent” throughout the Undergraduate Teaching Assessment, and was among the first “Excellent Universities of Undergraduate Education” in the country. In the same year, a national science park was established with the authorization of State Ministry of Technology and State MOE. In the year of 2000, the School was accepted to be established. In 2001, the new cooperative construction of State MOE and Guangdong Province was enforced in SCUT and it was listed among the highest universities in the country.
After sixty years of development, the university has become a multidisciplinary university that specializes in engineering, combined with science, conjointly promoting well coordinated development of management, economics, humanities and law. With actual knowledge pursuit, the school eminent spirit of concord and truthfulness has come into being, that helped cultivating exclusive technological and management skills that are welcomed to the society.
In recent years, the demand of graduate students has exceeded their supply. The employment rate of its undergraduates in 2013 has reached 99.33% which of its graduates 99.48%. Since its innovation, the university has cultivated for the country over 290,000 graduates in the least levels. Many school fellows have become technological mainstays, distinguished entrepreneurs or leading cadres.
Facing the new century, SCUT will take discipline constructions as necessity, regard individual of its roots, take into account talent cultivation for its final aim and highlight technological pursuit. The school intends to enlarge and extend to understand the coordinated development of its scale, structure, quality and benefit.
South China University of Technology accelerates its education rebuild by power train the university from multi discipline-oriented to integration-oriented, from teaching research-oriented to research-oriented, and from partial open type to utterly open form, thus on build SCUT into a top-level State university and known world-class university.
